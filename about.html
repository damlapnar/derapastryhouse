Our Story

D'era House was born out of a simple dream — to bring the joy of handmade, authentic pastries to our community. What started as a humble kitchen passion turned into a beloved local bakery in Clifton, NJ, known for quality, care, and creativity in every single bite.

Woman-Owned & Community-Rooted

D'era House is proudly woman-owned and operated. We believe that a great bakery isn't just about great food — it's about the people you serve and the connections you build. Every time you walk through our doors or open one of our boxes, we want you to feel that warmth.

Our Vision

We believe that pastries aren't just desserts — they are celebrations of life's sweetest moments. At D'era, every cheesecake, cookie, and baklava is crafted with the intention to spread happiness and connection. We use the finest ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of heart.

From the Heart

"This bakery is more than just a business. It's the extension of everything we love: flavors that remind us of home, moments that feel warm, and a commitment to making people smile."
